LIFE OF HARRIET

Hello, my name is Harriet, and I'm a 22-year-old entrepreneur. In 2019, I started learning to code because I was not rich. I reestablished contact with a friend who works in technology. He explained to me the advantages of working in the industry and how I could have a fulfilling life even if I worked from home. I got inspired at once. A solution to my main issue, which was money. Due to my involvement in school and an internship, I attempted to learn how to code twice before giving up. Because my supportive friends constantly encouraged me to pursue my interests, my desire remained intact. Being surrounded by people who were using technology in some way and having the desire still there.I chose front end development because of the beauty and simplicity I could provide with just a few lines of code. It seemed like I was practically working magic.

Hello, I'm Harriet and I'm thrilled you stopped by. I'm an entrepreneur and also a student of UDACITY and Alt School Africa. Lately, I have been so passionate about Front End Development. I have been trying to build projects while serving my country as a corper and also still handling my business.
